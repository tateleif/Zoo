1. Bird watching

Have you ever wondered what kinds of birds live in your backyard? Let's take a look! All you need is some binoculars, a notepad or notebook and a pencil.



Find a window or shadowy area to do your work. Make sure you do not disturb, interrupt or surprise the birds.



Sit down and look through your binoculars. What do you see? Every time you see a bird, mark it down on your paper.
